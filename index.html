How VITA Works , For Tech Enthusiasts

Local Data Layer

All personal health data is stored locally on your VITA device using encrypted storage. This includes meal logs, activity patterns, biometric data, and personal preferences. No personal information leaves your home unless you explicitly choose to share anonymized summaries.

Intelligent Query Interface

Natural language processing happens locally when possible, with a lightweight LLM for speech recognition and basic queries. For complex requests, anonymized queries are sent to cloud AI, with responses returned to your device for local processing and storage.

Automated Nutrient Processing

Using and voice input, VITA automatically identifies food items and estimates portion sizes. This data is processed locally to track nutrition against your personal goals, with all calculations happening on-device for maximum privacy.

Does VITA require an internet connection?

Yes. While your health database is stored locally for privacy, VITA uses advanced cloud-based LLMs to understand natural speech and provide intelligent cooking guidance. Your meal history, personal preferences, and long-term health data remain securely on your device.
